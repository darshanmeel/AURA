The problem
02 / 07

Your agents keep a diary. Nobody reads it.

Every Claude Code session is written to disk — every prompt, model call, token count, tool invocation, file edit.

It's a goldmine. And almost nobody looks, because it's unreadable, there's no total, and there's no memory once a session scrolls off.

What Aura is
03 / 07

Local-first analytics for AI-coding-agent sessions.

It watches your transcripts, transforms them with dbt, and surfaces cost, productivity, and pipeline-health signals through a dashboard.

Everything runs in Docker on your own machine. No data leaves your laptop. MIT-licensed.
