What Aura is
02 / 06

Local-first analytics for AI-coding-agent sessions.

It reads the JSONL transcripts your agents already write, transforms them with dbt, and surfaces cost, attribution, and pipeline health.

Everything runs in Docker on your own machine. Nothing leaves your laptop. MIT-licensed and open.
